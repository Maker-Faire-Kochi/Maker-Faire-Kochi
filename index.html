About

A festival for makers, by makers.

Maker Faire Kochi is a community-driven celebration of creativity and innovation — a place where engineers, artists, students, hobbyists, and tinkerers come together to share what they've built. Whether it's a 3D-printed prosthetic, a homemade synthesizer, a robot that draws, or a kinetic sculpture made from scrap — if you made it, it belongs here.
